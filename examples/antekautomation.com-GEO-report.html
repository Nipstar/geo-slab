What's actually broken

the problems

2 critical. 4 high priority. Here is what matters most.

01

No Wikipedia or Wikidata presence

The single strongest signal for AI entity recognition is absent. All 9 AI platforms use Wikipedia/Wikidata for entity verification. Without it, AI models lack confidence that Antek Automation is a real, notable entity.

02

Brand authority at 28/100 — near-invisible entity footprint

YouTube (6 subscribers), LinkedIn (1 follower), Reddit (negligible), no Trustpilot, no G2. Your technical infrastructure is excellent but AI models can't verify who you are through third-party signals.

03

Google Business Profile categories are wrong

Listed as 'manufacturer' and 'telecommunications_service_provider' instead of AI consultant or business automation service. GBP feeds directly into Gemini — wrong categories mean wrong query matching.

04

No blog or resource content

Zero articles, guides, or educational content. This limits topical authority, freshness signals, and citability surfaces across all platforms.

05

No IndexNow support

Bing Copilot and ChatGPT cannot discover content changes efficiently. A 30-minute fix on Vercel that immediately improves two platform scores.

06

Missing Content-Security-Policy header

The most significant missing security header. Required for enterprise client trust. Configurable via vercel.json.

07

Founder bio lacks substantiation

'30+ years in technology' is stated but not evidenced with specific roles, companies, or achievements. Detailed career history would significantly boost expertise signals.

08

Only one detailed case study visible

The Deccan Grill case study is excellent. Adding 2-3 more across trades, finance, and operations would demonstrate breadth of experience.

09

Organization schema missing recommended properties

No description, email, contactPoint, openingHoursSpecification, or priceRange on the LocalBusiness entity. Easy additions for rich result eligibility.
